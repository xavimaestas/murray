Murray, Utah

The historical industrious center of the Salt Lake Valley

Smelter

Murray was selected as the site for several smelters due to the successful mining in Park City and Little Cottonwood Canyon.

Workers

Workers came from all over the world to work at the Murray Smelters. People came mostly from Asia and Eastern Europe.

Power

Murray has had it's own municipal power since 1913. This helps to keep the cost of power low for the residents.
